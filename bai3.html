One particular book which is recommended reading is The Street Lawyer by John Grisham. This book is about a lawyer who begins re-evaluating his priorities in life when a bad incident occurs within his law firm. Consequently, he becomes acquainted with the inner city streets, and realizes the harsh existence of the homeless, and vows to give them a chance in the courts.The Street Lawyer is a great book.It is well written and interesting. Others books by John Grisham include The Film, The Pelican Brief,and The Client.
